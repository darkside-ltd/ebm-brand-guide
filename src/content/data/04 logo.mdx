## Primary Logo

Primary branding includes sponsorship, and comes in two distinct flavours for use in different contexts to ensure the logo shape can be made out. 
<div class="frame bg-4 spaced">
    <Imag src={img2} alt="Primary Logo." format="svg" />
</div>

The Horizontal logo should be used where the sizing and spacing rules would make the primary logo illegible, such as any canvas like letter banners with a ratio of 4:1 or more.

</div>

## Logo Sizing

For normal use the primary logo should be at least 80px wide. 

<div class="frame bg-4 spaced">
    <Imag src={img8} alt="Size Guide." format="svg" />
</div>

The horizontal logo should be at least 160px wide.

    <Imag src={img9} alt="Horizontal Size Guide." format="svg" />

## Alternative Logo

The alternative logo is reserved for use cases where it would be otherwise impossible to meet WCAG accessibility standards or when a white / light background is essential.
